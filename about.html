Meet the Owner:

Blake French

Behind every Millennium Pool is the vision and expertise of our founder, Blake French. With decades of experience and a lifelong dedication to craftsmanship, Blake has established himself as one of the most respected names in the pool and outdoor living industry.

A graduate of multiple Genesis 3 Design Group programs, considered the highest standard in pool design education, Blake has studied under some of the world’s leading experts in watershape design, architecture, structural engineering, and advanced hydraulics. This deep technical training, combined with his creative eye, allows him to approach every project with both artistry and precision.

Blake’s commitment to continuous learning has taken him through specialized courses in areas ranging from soils and geology to water purification and color theory. He is also a Certified Pool Operator (C.P.O.), a graduate of Teledyne Laars Technical Service School, and an active member of respected industry associations including the Association of Pool & Spa Professionals and the Genesis 3 Group of Professional Watershape Designers.

His work and leadership have earned Millennium Pools numerous awards, including Gold Medal recognition at the Southwest Pool and Spa Show, as well as national features in Luxury Pools Magazine, Waterways Lifestyle Magazine, Redbook, Woman’s Day, and Good Housekeeping.
